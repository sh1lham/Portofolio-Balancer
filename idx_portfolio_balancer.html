How rebalancing works
1  Set target weights for each asset (e.g. BBCA = 35%)
2  Monitor live prices — portfolio weights shift as prices move
3  When drift exceeds threshold, calculate trade to restore weights
4  Execute buy/sell orders to bring portfolio back on target
